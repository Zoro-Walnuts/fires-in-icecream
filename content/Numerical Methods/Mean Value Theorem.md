# Mean Value Theorem
> [!tldr] Formula:
> $$
> \frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a} = f'(\epsilon)
> $$
> $$
> F(x) = f(x)- f(a) - \frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}(x-1)
> $$

## Example
$f(x) = x^3$
$f'(x) = 3x^2$
$f(-2) = -8; f(2) = 8$
$3c^2 = \frac{8-(-8)}{2-(-2)} = \frac{16}{4} = 4$
$c^2 = \frac{4}{3}$
$c = \frac{2}{\sqrt{3}} = \frac{2 \sqrt{3}}{3}$
